==========
CHARACTERS
==========
Jay Gatsby - a young, mysterious millionaire with shady business connections. Originally from North Dakota.
During World War I, when he was a young military officer stationed at the United States Army's Camp Taylor in
Louisville, Kentucky, he encountered the love of his life, the beautiful debutante Daisy Buchanan.

Nick Carraway - Our narrator and guide throughout the story.

Daisy Buchanan - a beautiful woman from Louisville, Kentucky, who made once in love with Gatsby.

Thomas "Tom" Buchanan - a millionaire who lives in East Egg, and Daisy's husband.

Myrtle Wilson - Tom's lover, whose lifeless husband George owns a run-down garage in the valley of ashes.
Myrtle herself possess a fierce vitality and desperately looks for way to improve her situation.
